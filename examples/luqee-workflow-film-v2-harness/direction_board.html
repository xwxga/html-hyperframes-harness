05 / Style Critique

05 / 风格检查

Design critique for the director

给导演看的设计检查

layout-01

Unity: intentionally messy. Rhythm: good opening drag. Text density: high but acceptable for problem beat.

统一性：刻意混乱。节奏：开场拖拽感成立。文字密度：高，但适合问题段。

layout-02

Unity: strongest board moment. Motion: reveal should be slower than card alignment.

统一性：全板最强时刻。动效：揭示应比卡片对齐更慢。

layout-03

Landing: clean. Risk: avoid adding feature claims at the end.

落点：干净。风险：结尾不要新增功能论点。
