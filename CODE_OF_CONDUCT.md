# Code of Conduct

## Standard

Contributors should keep discussion technical, specific, and useful. Critique artifacts, contracts, and implementation decisions directly; do not attack people.

## Expected Behavior

- Keep issues and reviews focused on reproducible facts.
- Respect the project scope and static V1 boundary.
- Do not post secrets, private data, or private planning state.
- Keep public discussion useful for people trying to reuse the harness.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments that derail technical work, expose secrets, or introduce personal attacks.
